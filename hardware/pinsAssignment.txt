SD Card Interface:
	SDIO_CK  - PB15
	SDIO_CMD - PA6
	SDIO_D0  - PB7
	SDIO_D1  - PA8
	SDIO_D2  - PA9
	SDIO_D3  - PB5

FC Interface:
	UART1_TX/SPI1_NSS  - PA15
	UART1_RX/SPI1_SCK  - PA3
	SPI1_MISO          - PA4
	SPI1_MOSI          - PA7

Status LED:
	Active LOW - PB9

BOOT Interface:
	BOOT0 - 10k pull-down, pad for jumpering to +3V3
	BOOT1 - 10k pull-down

Bootloader Interface:
	RX_SCK - shared with SWDIO
	MOSI   - shared with SWCLK
	NRST

HW Rev ID:
	Rev A: PB12 PB13 PB14 - All LOW
